Planning and Preparation

Planning

The first part of a trip is planning. The planning phase is one of the most important phases before going on a trip. First things first you have to know where it is that you want to go. Places you dreamed of seeing are now completely available to you. Rules don't mean worth a damn if you don't get caught. Next pack only the items you value most for this kind of adventure. If your going up a mountain pack an ice pick. If your going down a river pack an oar. If your surviving the wilderness well pack 5 pounds of rice and a lot of ammunition. Make sure you know anything about the surrounding area. I learned the hard way when I crossed a river and couldn't escape to the other side when it counted most. Finally make sure you have no regrets, doing something you hate for no reason is the worst way to spend your life. Make sure you go out with a bang. Make every second count. There isn't much to it, hell you could probably leave right now. Make the most of every second, and do it so that in the end you can look upon that time and write your story with your pen.

Preparation

Preparation is another important part of this whole ordeal. Make sure you a ready to leave with or without warning should the need arise. Personally, when attempting to achieve freedom I find it is easier to drop the things holding you back such as Money, and or obligations. When I started my trip around America I gave all my money to charity. I incentivize you to do the same. Those people need that money more than I. You will also need to break ties from Work and loved ones. One of those will be significantly harder than the other. There are many strategies to accomplish this feat, however, none of them are easy to do. Once you are ready set out with the hardest way possible. Walk a thousand miles if you must because eventually, you will make it to your destination.
